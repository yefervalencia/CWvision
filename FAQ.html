Frequently Asked Questions

What is CWvision?

CWvision is a platform designed to help clients and workers manage home renovation projects in real-time. It provides tools for tracking progress, uploading images, and communicating effectively.

How do I register?

You can register by clicking on the "Get started" button on the homepage or navigation bar. Fill out the registration form with your details and select your role (client, worker, or admin).

Can I track the progress of my project?

Yes, clients can track the progress of their projects in real-time through the dashboard. Workers can update the status and upload images to keep clients informed.

Is there a mobile app?

Currently, CWvision is only available as a web platform. A mobile app may be developed in future updates.

How do I contact support?

You can reach out to our support team through the "Contact Us" page. We are available to assist you with any questions or issues.
